Why India is one of the only countries that has maintained its culture:

  1. Lack of financial capacity to change at a faster rate
    • - High Population
    • - High Pollution
  2. Traditional belief system that things are as they are for a reason
    • - This is how people conformed to castes for so long
    • - Many people's families stayed in low position jobs for many years
  3. The traditions are undeniably beautiful
    • - They are becoming more and more popular with media
    • - The Kardashians attended an Indian wedding
